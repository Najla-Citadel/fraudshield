# MCMC Presentation Draft: FraudShield

- **Headline:** FraudShield: Unified National Scam Prevention
- **Subheadline:** Leveraging Intelligence to Protect the Malaysian Digital Ecosystem
- **Presenter:** [USER NAME/COMPANY]
- **Theme:** Professional, Modern, Trustworthy (Dark/Blue palette)

## Slide 2: The Challenge
- **Headline:** The Evolving Scam Landscape in Malaysia
- **Key Points:**
    - **Macau Scams & Phishing:** Rapid movement of illicit funds.
    - **Mule Accounts:** High volume of temporary accounts used for laundering.
    - **Communication Gaps:** Fraudsters exploit the delay between reporting and blacklisting.
    - **Public Fatigue:** Users overwhelmed by generic warnings; need real-time, context-aware protection.

## Slide 3: The Solution
- **Headline:** Unified Multi-Modal Protection
- **Content:**
    - **Real-Time Caller ID:** Identifying known scam numbers during ringing.
    - **URL Security Hub:** Deep-link analysis for SMS and WhatsApp phishing.
    - **Document & APK Scan:** detecting malicious payloads in attachments.
    - **Behavioral Intervention:** Post-call safety checks and cooling-off periods for high-risk interactions.

## Slide 4: Intelligence Engine
- **Headline:** Data-Driven Risk Assessment
- **Key Points:**
    - **Community Intelligence:** Crowd-sourced reports with weighted trust scores.
    - **Technical Heuristics:** Offline pattern recognition (Neighbor spoofing, obfuscated URLs).
    - **Official Synergy (The Goal):** Integration of PDRM Semak Mule & MCMC Malicious URL lists.
    - **Result:** A dynamic 0-100 risk score with transparent justification for the user.

## Slide 5: Technical Excellence
- **Headline:** Built for Speed and Security
- **Content:**
    - **Secure-by-Design:** Anti-replay middleware, certificate pinning, and deterministic encryption.
    - **Performance:** Node.js/Express backend capable of sub-500ms risk lookups.
    - **Cross-Platform:** Flutter architecture ensures rapid deployment to Android and (future) iOS.
    - **Modern Infrastructure:** Docker-ready, Redis-backed rate limiting, and Bull queue for intelligence sync.

## Slide 6: Vision & Roadmap
- **Headline:** A Phased Approach to National Safety
- **Content:**
    - **Phase 1-2 (Current):** Core detection completeness and baseline police API integration.
    - **Phase 3-4:** AI/ML enhancements, OCR for scam image detection, and voice analysis.
    - **Phase 5 (Scaling):** Telco & Bank API partnerships for automated reporting and fund freezing.

## Slide 7: The Collaboration Ask
- **Headline:** Empowering the National Scam Response Center (NSRC)
- **Call to Action:**
    - **API Agreement:** Direct, real-time access to MCMC/PDRM threat feeds.
    - **Reporting Bridge:** Automatic submission of FraudShield community reports to official channels.
    - **Unified Registry:** Building a more comprehensive blacklist together to protect every Malaysian.
