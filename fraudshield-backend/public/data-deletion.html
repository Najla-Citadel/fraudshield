Account Deletion Request

We're sorry to see you go. If you would like to delete your FraudShield account and all associated personal data, please follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Draft an email to support@citadel.com (or your support email).
  2. Include the email address associated with your FraudShield account.
  3. Write "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line.
  4. Our team will process your request within 7 business days.

What happens to your data?

Deleted Data

  • Full Name & Profile
  • Email Address
  • Scam Report History
  • Points & Badges

Retained Data

  • Transaction Records (for fraud prevention)
  • Subscription History (for financial records)
